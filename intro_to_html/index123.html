The Legend of Zelda

Welcome to the Legend of Zelda fan page! This website mainly focuses on The legend of zelda ocarina of time game. The legend of zelda oot(ocarina of time) came out in 1998 on the nintendo 64. It is an action adventure game that takes place in the mytical land of hyrule. You take control of the titular character link and go on a quest to save zelda the princess of Hyrule. Along the way you will make friends meet foes and uncover lots of secrets.

story

The story takes place in Hyrule during a peaceful time. The player takes control of the character link when he is a child. He is summoned by the Great Duku tree. After speaking to the awesome tree link must set out to the castle of Hyrule to speak to the princess where the story unfolds.

gameplay

The gameplay is great there is alot of movement for the character. Your able to roll forward striff left and right and backflip when locked on to an enemy. Your player will unlock many different wepons and tools that will help him along his journey.
